Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation Policy

The RKRSSS app strictly prohibits any content or activity that promotes, enables, or condones child sexual abuse or exploitation. We are committed to ensuring a safe digital environment for all users and comply fully with Google Play's Families policy, COPPA, and other applicable child protection laws.

Any user found violating these standards will be reported to the appropriate authorities, and their access will be permanently removed.

If you believe someone is violating these terms or you find concerning content, please contact us immediately at: support@yourapp.com
